Presentation Of The Projet

Project History

The project to develop our horror game was initiated by Naël, our project manager, who brought together a team around a common vision: to create an immersive and emotional experience in the slasher genre. Inspired by iconic titles such as “Resident Evil” and “Stay Out of the House”, we decided to merge our programming, scriptwriting and sound design to create a game that immerses players in a dark, nostalgic atmosphere, reminiscent of games from the PS1 period.

Production schedule

Here are the main steps:

  1. Pre-production phase (Semester 1): Story research and development, Creation of logo prototypes and initial design, Establishment of functional and technical requirements.
  2. Production phase (Semester 2): Development of the game engine on Unity 3D, Programming of game mechanics and creation of graphics, Integration of sound elements and gameplay testing.
  3. Post-production phase: Game testing and debugging, Creation of marketing material (trailers, website), Preparation for distribution and launch.

Problems encountered

During the project, we encountered the following challenges :

  • Member availability: Team members have personal and professional commitments which limit their availability.
  • Mastery of tools: Some members had difficulties with modeling and programming tools.
  • Artistic coherence: Ensuring coherence in the graphic and sound worlds was a challenge.
  • Lack of technical equipment: Some group members had problems with their computer equipment.

Envisaged solutions

  • Flexible scheduling: Setting up a flexible work schedule to accommodate everyone's availability.
  • Training and tutorials: Organization of training sessions and sharing of resources to improve tool mastery.
  • Regular meetings: Weekly meetings to discuss progress and adjustments needed to maintain artistic coherence.
